Most of us have money in more than one place — a checking account here, a savings account there, maybe a credit card with a different bank. Jumping between apps makes it almost impossible to see what's actually happening with your money as a whole.

Fiverse solves this with the **Unified View**: a single, real-time snapshot of every account you connect, all in one place. Think of it as your personal financial command center.

## How to link your first account

<Steps>
  <Step title="Open Fiverse and tap Home">
    Launch the app and tap **Home** in the bottom navigation bar.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Tap + Add Account">
    Tap the **+ Add Account** button at the top of your dashboard.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Find your bank">
    Search for your bank by name or scroll through the list of supported institutions.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Log in securely">
    Enter your bank credentials in the secure, encrypted connection screen.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Select your accounts">
    Choose which accounts to include — checking, savings, credit cards — then tap **Confirm**.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Repeat for other banks">
    Add as many banks or accounts as you like. More connections = clearer picture.
  </Step>
</Steps>

Once linked, your **Total Balance** on the Home screen reflects the combined view of everything — updated automatically every time you open the app.

<Tip>
  The more accounts you connect, the more accurately your Financial Copilot can understand your full financial picture and give you meaningful guidance.
</Tip>

<Note>
  Fiverse does **not** store your bank login credentials. Your connection uses encrypted, read-only access — Fiverse can see your transactions but can never move your money.
</Note>
