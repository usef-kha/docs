The Analytics screen is your financial mirror. It shows you not just what you spend, but how your habits are changing over time — which is where the real insight lives.

## The Net Cash Flow panel

At the top of the Analytics screen, three tiles tell the essential story at a glance:

<CardGroup cols={3}>
  <Card title="Income" icon="circle-arrow-down" color="#22c55e">
    Total money that came in this month across all connected accounts.
  </Card>
  <Card title="Expenses" icon="circle-arrow-up" color="#ef4444">
    Total money that went out this month across all categories.
  </Card>
  <Card title="Net Savings" icon="piggy-bank" color="#3B6FE8">
    The difference — how much you actually kept this month.
  </Card>
</CardGroup>

**Example:** If your Income shows €3,500 and Expenses show €2,480, your Net Savings tile shows **€1,020**. This single number tells you instantly whether you moved forward financially this month.

## The Monthly Trends chart

Below the tiles, a bar chart shows your spending month by month.

- **Blue bar** = current month (highlighted)
- **Grey bars** = previous months
- A **percentage indicator** (e.g., `+6.4% vs last month`) appears next to the current total

A growing bar means spending is rising. A shrinking bar means you successfully cut back.

<Tip>
  Tap any bar in the chart to see a full breakdown of that month's spending by category — great for pinpointing exactly what drove a spike.
</Tip>

<Tip>
  Check your Spending Trends at the start of each month. Just 60 seconds reviewing last month is one of the most powerful habits you can build for long-term financial clarity.
</Tip>
