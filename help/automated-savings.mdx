The biggest challenge with saving isn't knowing you should do it — it's the moment-to-moment discipline of actually doing it. That's why Fiverse includes an **Automated Savings engine** that removes willpower from the equation entirely.

## How it works

Once you set a savings goal and activate a savings plan, your Financial Copilot analyses your cash flow and schedules automatic contributions toward your goal. It's designed to move money at the right time — typically just after your regular income arrives — so you **save first and spend what's left**, rather than the other way around.

## Activating automated savings

<Steps>
  <Step title="Open a goal">
    Go to **Goals** and tap the goal you want to automate.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Tap Create Savings Plan">
    Find the **AI Suggestions** panel and tap **Create Savings Plan**.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Review and adjust">
    Review the suggested monthly contribution and adjust if needed.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Confirm the schedule">
    Choose the account to draw from and confirm the schedule.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Activate">
    Tap **Activate**. Your Financial Copilot handles the rest.
  </Step>
</Steps>

<Note>
  Every automated contribution appears in the **Recent Contributions** list on the Goals screen — a transparent record of every amount saved and when.
</Note>
