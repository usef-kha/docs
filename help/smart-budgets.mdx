A budget should feel like a helpful guide — not a strict punishment. Smart Budgets in Fiverse are flexible, realistic, and simple to set up. You decide how much you want to spend in each category every month, and Fiverse quietly tracks your progress in the background.

## Setting up your category budgets

<Steps>
  <Step title="Go to Budgets">
    Tap **Budgets** in the bottom navigation bar.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Review your Monthly Overview">
    You'll see your **Monthly Budget Overview** — total budget, amount spent so far, and what's remaining.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Add a category limit">
    Scroll to **Category Budgets** and tap **+** next to any category to set a monthly spending limit.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Enter your target">
    Set an amount for categories like Groceries, Utilities, Dining, Transport, or a custom category of your choice.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Save and go">
    Tap **Save**. Your budget is now live and tracking automatically.
  </Step>
</Steps>

## Understanding the budget indicators

Each category shows a progress bar that fills as you spend.

| Indicator | Meaning |
|-----------|---------|
| 🟢 Green bar | You're on track with comfortable room left. |
| 🟡 Yellow warning | You're approaching your limit — a gentle heads-up. |
| 🔴 Red at 100% | You've reached your budget for this category this month. |

<Tip>
  Not sure where to start? Tap the **Auto** toggle next to Category Budgets to let your Financial Copilot suggest amounts based on your actual spending history. It's a great starting point you can adjust from there.
</Tip>
