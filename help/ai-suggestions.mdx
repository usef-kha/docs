Once your goal is set, your Financial Copilot doesn't just sit back and watch. It analyses your income, spending, and target date — then gives you specific, actionable guidance to make it happen.

## Smart Advice: your monthly savings target

Inside each goal, the **AI Suggestions** panel shows a clear, no-jargon recommendation:

<Frame>
  <div style={{ padding: "16px", borderLeft: "4px solid #3B6FE8", background: "#F0F4FF", borderRadius: "4px", fontStyle: "italic" }}>
    "To reach your goal by Dec 15, 2025, try saving €185 per month."
  </div>
</Frame>

This number is calculated from the gap between your current savings and your target, spread across the months remaining. It removes all the guesswork and gives you one simple number to aim for.

Tap **Create Savings Plan** to activate it and Fiverse will help you put that amount aside each month automatically.

## What If? scenarios: see the impact of saving a little more

The second card in the AI Suggestions panel shows how small changes make a big difference:

<Frame>
  <div style={{ padding: "16px", borderLeft: "4px solid #5B8EF0", background: "#F0F4FF", borderRadius: "4px", fontStyle: "italic" }}>
    "If you increase your monthly savings by €50, you could reach your goal 1 month earlier!"
  </div>
</Frame>

Tap **Adjust Goal** to experiment with different savings amounts and see exactly how each one affects your timeline. You're always in control of what you commit to.

<Tip>
  The What If? tool is especially useful after a pay rise, a bonus, or after cancelling unused subscriptions. Feed that extra money into your goal and watch the timeline shrink.
</Tip>
