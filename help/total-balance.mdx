At the top of your Home screen, your **Total Balance** shows the combined balance across all your connected accounts right now. This is your financial north star.

## Understanding the balance display

<CardGroup cols={2}>
  <Card title="Total Balance" icon="wallet">
    The sum of all connected accounts — checking, savings, and credit. A positive number means you have more in savings than you owe on credit cards.
  </Card>
  <Card title="Month-over-Month Change" icon="arrow-trend-up">
    A percentage showing how your total shifted compared to last month. Green means you moved forward. Red means it declined.
  </Card>
  <Card title="Savings Snapshot" icon="bullseye-arrow">
    Your current progress toward active goals — so your future and present are always visible together.
  </Card>
  <Card title="Recent Transactions" icon="list">
    Your latest activity across all connected accounts, automatically categorised in real time.
  </Card>
</CardGroup>

## Reading the month-over-month indicator

The percentage below your balance is one of the most useful signals in the app:

- **`+6.2% vs last month` in green** → Your financial position improved this month. Your net savings increased.
- **`-3.1% vs last month` in red** → Your position declined this month. A signal to review your spending.

This single indicator tells you instantly whether your habits this month moved you forward or backward — no spreadsheet required.

## Recent Transactions

The Recent Transactions list shows your latest activity across all connected accounts. Transactions are automatically categorised (e.g., Starbucks Coffee → Dining; Uber → Transport), making your history searchable and filterable from the Analytics screen.

This real-time feed is your first line of defence against unexpected charges and helps you stay aware of daily spending without logging into individual bank apps.

<Tip>
  Your Total Balance updates automatically every time you open Fiverse. Keep the app connected to the internet so background syncs can run and your picture stays accurate.
</Tip>
