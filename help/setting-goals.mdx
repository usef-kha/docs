title: "Setting Active Goals"
description: "Create a goal, set a target date, and let your Financial Copilot do the tracking."
---

Having a clear goal changes everything. When you know what you're saving for — a trip to Seoul, a new laptop, or a six-month emergency fund — every spending decision suddenly has context. Here's how to get started.

## How to create your first goal

<Steps>
  <Step title="Go to Goals">
    Tap **Goals** in the bottom navigation bar.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Tap + New Goal">
    Hit the **+ New Goal** button.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Name your goal">
    Give it something meaningful — "Trip to Seoul", "New Laptop", "Emergency Fund".
  </Step>
  <Step title="Enter your target amount">
    Set the total amount you need to save.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Set a target date">
    Pick the date you want to reach your goal.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Choose a category and save">
    Select a category (Travel, Savings, Gadget, etc.) and tap **Save**.
  </Step>
</Steps>

Your goal will now appear on the Goals screen showing:

- How much you've saved so far
- How much remains
- A countdown to your target date
- A progress bar that fills as you contribute

<Tip>
  You can have multiple active goals running at the same time. Your Financial Copilot tracks each one independently and keeps you updated on progress across all of them.
</Tip>
