The **AI Insights** section on your Home screen updates daily. These are short, focused notifications designed to flag anything worth your attention without overwhelming you with data.

## Types of insights you'll see

<AccordionGroup>
  <Accordion title="⚡ Spending Spikes" icon="bolt" defaultOpen>
    If your spending in a particular category jumps significantly compared to your normal patterns, your Financial Copilot flags it immediately.

    > *"Dining Expenses Rising — your restaurant spending increased 23% this month."*

    This isn't a scolding — it's a heads-up so you can decide whether to adjust for the rest of the month.
  </Accordion>

  <Accordion title="💡 Optimization Tips" icon="lightbulb">
    Proactive suggestions to improve your financial health — triggered when your Financial Copilot spots a clear opportunity.

    > *"Optimize Your Subscriptions — you could save €47/month by cancelling unused subscriptions."*
  </Accordion>

  <Accordion title="📈 Performance Updates" icon="chart-line">
    Positive news matters too. Your Financial Copilot celebrates goal progress, highlights months where net savings improved, and reminds you how far you've come.
  </Accordion>
</AccordionGroup>

## How insights are prioritised

Each insight is tagged with a priority level so you always know what deserves attention most:

| Priority | Example |
|----------|---------|
| 🔴 High | A budget category has reached 100% |
| 🟡 Medium | Dining expenses rising vs. last month |
| 🟢 Low | General optimisation suggestions |

High-priority insights appear at the top. Tap any card to expand it and see the full detail, along with a recommended action. Dismiss insights you've already acted on to keep your dashboard clean.

<Tip>
  Check your AI Insights first thing when you open Fiverse. Even a 30-second scan each morning builds the awareness habit that makes the biggest long-term difference to how you manage money.
</Tip>
