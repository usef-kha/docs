The first sync is where the magic starts. Fiverse reads through your recent transaction history and begins making sense of your financial life — automatically, in seconds.

## What Fiverse analyses during setup

<CardGroup cols={2}>
  <Card title="Income Patterns" icon="arrow-trend-up">
    Identifies regular deposits to understand how much money comes in and when.
  </Card>
  <Card title="Spending Categories" icon="tags">
    Every transaction is automatically sorted — Groceries, Utilities, Dining, Transport, Subscriptions, and more.
  </Card>
  <Card title="Recurring Charges" icon="repeat">
    Flags regular payments, especially any that may be unfamiliar or forgotten, so you can review them straight away.
  </Card>
  <Card title="Spending Trends" icon="chart-bar">
    Compares recent months to establish a baseline and highlight where your habits are shifting.
  </Card>
</CardGroup>

## What to expect

The initial analysis usually completes within a few seconds. You will see a progress indicator on your Home screen while the sync is running. Once done, your dashboard populates with your first insights — and your Financial Copilot will already have a few things to tell you.

<Note>
  Fiverse uses encrypted, read-only access to your accounts. It can see your transactions but can never move your money.
</Note>
